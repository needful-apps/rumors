All About Cows

Introduction

Cows are domesticated bovine animals. They are commonly raised for their meat, milk, and hides, and they also play an important role in agriculture.

Characteristics

Cows are known for their large size, gentle demeanor, and characteristic "moo" sound. They have a four-chambered stomach, which helps them digest tough plant matter.

Interesting Facts

  • Cows can sleep standing up, but they can only dream lying down.
  • There are over 800 different breeds of cattle.
  • A cow's gestation period is about nine months, similar to humans.
